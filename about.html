Accelerating the World's Transition

Tesla represents the pinnacle of technology, engineering, and sustainable design. Our goal is to build a world powered by solar energy, running on batteries and transported by electric vehicles.

Our Mission

We build products that are beautiful, safe, and wildly fast. Every Tesla is designed to be the safest car in its class. With over-the-air updates, they get better over time, and our rapidly expanding Supercharger network means you can go anywhere.

Technology

From the silicon to the software, we design the compute and neural network systems necessary to enable our Autopilot and Full Self-Driving capabilities. We also build gigafactories that scale battery production globally.
